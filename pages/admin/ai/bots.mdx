# Chat Bots

### Step 1: Go to Chat Bots Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the "AI Setup" section.
2. Click on the "Chat Bots" tab.
[![image](/images/admin/ai/bots/1.png)](/images/admin/ai/bots/1.png)

### Step 2: Create Chat Bot
1. Click on the "Create Chat Bot" button.
[![image](/images/admin/ai/bots/2.png)](/images/admin/ai/bots/2.png)
2. Enter the chat bot details.
3. Click on the "Save" button.
[![image](/images/admin/ai/bots/3.png)](/images/admin/ai/bots/3.png)

    [![image](/images/admin/ai/bots/3-1.png)](/images/admin/ai/bots/3-1.png)

### Step 3: Edit Chat Bot
1. Click on the "Edit" button to edit the chat bot.
[![image](/images/admin/ai/bots/4.png)](/images/admin/ai/bots/4.png)
2. Edit the chat bot details.
3. Click on the "Update Changes" button.
[![image](/images/admin/ai/bots/5.png)](/images/admin/ai/bots/5.png)

    [![image](/images/admin/ai/bots/5-1.png)](/images/admin/ai/bots/5-1.png)

### Step 4: Delete Chat Bot
1. Click on the "Delete" button to delete the chat bot.
[![image](/images/admin/ai/bots/6.png)](/images/admin/ai/bots/6.png)

### Step 5: Bulk Delete Chat Bots
1. Select the chat bots you want to delete or click on the "Select All" checkbox to select all the chat bots.
[![image](/images/admin/ai/bots/7.png)](/images/admin/ai/bots/7.png)
