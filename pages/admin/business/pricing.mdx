# Subscription Plans

Here you can see the subscription plans that you have created.

### Step 1: Go to Payment Gateways Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the "Business Setup" section.
2. Click on the "Plan & Pricing" tab.
[![image](/images/admin/business/pricing/1.png)](/images/admin/business/pricing/1.png)

### Step 2: Create Subscription Plan
1. Click on the "Create Plan" button.
[![image](/images/admin/business/pricing/2.png)](/images/admin/business/pricing/2.png)
2. Enter the plan name, price, description and other details.
3. Click on the "Save" button.
[![image](/images/admin/business/pricing/3.png)](/images/admin/business/pricing/3.png)

    [![image](/images/admin/business/pricing/4.png)](/images/admin/business/pricing/4.png)

### Step 3: Edit Subscription Plan
1. Click on the "Edit" button to edit the plan.
[![image](/images/admin/business/pricing/5.png)](/images/admin/business/pricing/5.png)
2. Edit the plan details.
3. Click on the "Update Changes" button.
[![image](/images/admin/business/pricing/6.png)](/images/admin/business/pricing/6.png)
