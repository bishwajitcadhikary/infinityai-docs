# Payment Gateways

### Step 1: Go to Payment Gateways Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the "Payment Gateways" section.
[![image](/images/admin/business/payments/1.png)](/images/admin/business/payments/1.png)
2. To set your payment gateway credentials, click on the "Edit" button next to the payment gateway you want to configure.

    For this example, we will be using the "Stripe" payment gateway.
[![image](/images/admin/business/payments/2.png)](/images/admin/business/payments/2.png)

### Step 2: Set your Payment Gateway Credentials
1. Enter your payment gateway credentials.
2. Change sandbox mode to "No" if you want to accept real payments.
[![image](/images/admin/business/payments/3.png)](/images/admin/business/payments/3.png)
